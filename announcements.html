Tribal Community Meeting: Friday 07/12/2024

All tribal members welcome. Meeting to start at 3:30 pm located at the PRTH office. 20300 Fir street
The Pit River Tribal Council and the Pit River Tribal Housing Board have decided todevelop new residential housing units on Tribal properties in Burney, Ca. The Housing Board is applying for a HUD grant to start the project. Bellow you will find a file for a draft layout of the housing development. The board will be asking for two millon dollars from HUD and will commit one million dollars of its own funds to start the project.
